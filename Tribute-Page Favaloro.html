Dr. Rene Favaloro

Here´s a time line of Dr. Favaloro´s life

  • 1923 - Born in La Plata, Province of Buenos Aires.
  • 1949 - He graduated as a doctor at the National University of La Plata.
  • 1950 - He moved to Jacinto Arauz to temporarily replace the local doctor.
  • 1962 - He moves to the United States to specialize in thoracic surgery.
  • 1967 - He begins to study a technique for using the saphenous vein in coronary surgery.
  • 1971 - He returns to Argentina and operates at the Güemes private sanatorium.
  • 1975 - He founded the Favaloro Foundation.
  • 1980 - Performed the first coronary bypass using the great saphenous vein.
  • 1992 - Receives the Prince of Asturias Award for Scientific and Technical Research.
  • 2000 - He dies in Buenos Aires.

"We must work, work and work with passion. There will always be time for fruitful leisure, for the benefit of all. We have to make an effort to improve ourselves individually but understanding that we are part of a society that demands our participation”.

(Year 1993, at Glacier Park Lodge, Montana, United States, when he left a speech to the students).
